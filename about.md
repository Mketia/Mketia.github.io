      <h2>What this site is</h2>
      <p>The Margin is my personal blog. The name comes from the habit of writing in the margins of books — small notes, half-formed thoughts, questions without answers. That's the spirit here: not finished arguments but live thinking.</p>

      <p>I try to publish when I have something worth saying. The goal is quality over frequency. Most posts take several drafts and weeks of waiting to see if they still hold up.</p>
