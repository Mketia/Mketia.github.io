Every site starts somewhere. This is mine starting.

I've wanted a personal website for a long time. Not for reach, not for an audience, but for a place that is mine — a place to put things that deserve to exist somewhere more permanent than a social media feed.

## What this is

*The Margin* is named after the habit of writing in the margins of books. Small notes, questions without answers, half-formed reactions. That's the spirit here: not finished arguments, but live thinking.

I'll write about craft — the process of making things carefully, whether that's writing, building software, or learning a skill. I'll write about reading, about technology, about whatever I'm trying to figure out. The topics will follow the thinking, not the other way around.

## What this isn't

This isn't a professional blog, a content marketing play, or a place designed for SEO. There are no tracking pixels here, no newsletter signup popups, no requests to subscribe.

If something resonates, share it. If you want to get in touch, [the contact page is here](/contact/). That's the whole relationship model.

## A note on frequency

I'm not committing to a publishing schedule. Some months there will be multiple posts. Some months, none. The goal is quality over consistency. I'd rather go quiet for a stretch and come back with something worth reading than publish on cadence and produce noise.

Thanks for being here.
